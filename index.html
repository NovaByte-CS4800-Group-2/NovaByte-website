Overview

Disaster relief application to aid victims of wildfires. Users will be provided with valuable resources and connections to help recover from losses and find community support.

Goals

1. Allow victims to have a quick and easy recovery after an unexpected wildfire.

2. Bring people together during a time of need.

3. Encourage large businesses to give back to their community through a centralized platform.

Specifications

Our application will provide the following key features:

Chat: Allows users to communicate on a forum-based message board to stay apprised of alerts and news for their communities as well as provide information and words of encouragement for those enduring hardship.

Fundraising: Provides a platform for groups and businesses to host

Host-Evacuee Matching: Matches hosts willing to lend their homes to those in need of housing.

Resources: Informs users about discounts, deals, or offers provided by businesses in the community in response to recent disasters.

Assistance: Gives users access to a 24-hour AI chatbot to assist them and provide comprehensive answers to inquiries.
