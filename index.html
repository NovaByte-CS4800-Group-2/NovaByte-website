Overview

Wildfire response application to provide various levels of support for victims of wildfires. Through an interactive, chat-like interface, users will be able to communicate with individuals, companies, and small businesses willing to provide aid. Rather than having to search for assistance through different platforms, websites, and applications, our application provides a consolidated database that supports victims at a vulnerable time of need.

Goals

1. Allow victims to have a quick and easy recovery after an unexpected wildfire.

2. Bring people together during a time of need.

3. Encourage large businesses to give back to their community through a centralized platform.

Specifications

Our application will provide the following key features:

    Chat
  • Description: Allows users to communicate on a forum-based message board to stay apprised of alerts and news for their communities as well as provide information and words of encouragement for those enduring hardship.
  • Ex: A member of the community describes the wildfire's aftermath and posts an update on the presence of public safety officers.
    Fundraising
  • Description: Provides a platform for groups and businesses to host fundraisers focused on fire relief.
  • Ex: Dominos advertises that they will donate all the money they make on a certain day for fire relief.
    Host-Evacuee Matching
  • Description: Matches hosts willing to lend their homes to those in need of housing. Users will be able to select options based on their needs and be matched with a host willing to help.
  • Ex: An evacuee states the number of household members and pets they have and is matched with a host that can meet those requirements.
    Assistance
  • Description: Gives users access to a 24-hour AI chatbot to assist them and provide comprehensive answers to inquiries.
  • Ex: A user asks for resources in their area and instantly gets an answer to their inquiries.
